# Design Principles

## Governance first

Artifacts exist to formalize governance claims as executable structures.

## Signal discipline

Only essays with meaningful structural and engagement signal should enter the artifact pipeline.

## Legible authority

Each artifact must identify where authority resides and how it is constrained.

## Executable enforcement

An artifact should move a concept closer to testable enforcement, not just better description.

## Managed lifecycle

Artifacts require status, lineage, and revocation semantics.

## Redress as a design requirement

Where a model can fail or be contested, that contestability should be visible in structure and process.
