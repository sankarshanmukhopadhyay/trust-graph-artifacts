# Signal Model for Artifact Selection

This repository uses dataset-derived signals to determine which essays qualify for artifact creation.

---

## Primary Signal

### Engagement rate

This reflects:

- depth of resonance
- conceptual adoption
- likelihood that an essay contains a reusable primitive

---

## Tier Classification

| Tier | Engagement | Interpretation |
|------|-----------:|----------------|
| A | >16% | Strong artifact candidate |
| B | 10–16% | Conditional candidate |
| C | <10% | Narrative only for now |

---

## Secondary Signals

- subscribes as delegation intent
- sustained engagement across related essays
- thematic clustering across multiple posts
- evidence that a concept introduces a new control surface

---

## Principle

Not all ideas become artifacts.

Only ideas that:

- demonstrate structural potential
- show audience validation
- affect execution semantics

are promoted.

---

## Failure Condition

If selection is not constrained:

- the repository becomes a concept dump
- governance weakens
- artifacts lose operational meaning

Signal discipline is mandatory.
