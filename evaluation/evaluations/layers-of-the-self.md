## Framing

This essay treats identity as contextual projection rather than monolithic disclosure. Systems do not simply need to know who a subject is. They need to know which role, which attributes, and which disclosure boundary apply in the current interaction.

That can be formalized as an identity projection model with explicit linkage, policy, and revocation semantics.
